### II.A — The Phase Boundary: C_α (February 2026) → D(t) (April 2026)
**Lead: ~2 months**

**ERI (SALT, February 2026):**
C_α = ‖μ_g‖²/Tr(Σ_g) is defined as the gradient signal-to-noise ratio and established as the fundamental grokking phase indicator. The transition C_α = 1 is the phase boundary between memorization (C_α < 1, noise dominates, Möbius inversion on the basin poset fails to recover the true gradient direction) and generalization (C_α > 1, signal dominates, inversion converges). C_α = 1 is simultaneously λ₁(ℒ_JL) = 0 in SALT's spectral identification.

**SOTA (Wang et al. arXiv:2604.04655, April 2026):**
D(t) — the effective dimensionality of the gradient flow — crosses 1 at grokking onset. D(t) < 1 = sub-diffusive = memorization. D(t) > 1 = super-diffusive = generalization. arXiv:2604.16431 further identifies D = 1 as a renormalisation-group fixed point.

**Comparison:**
C_α and D(t) are not the same statistic — C_α measures gradient mean-to-covariance ratio; D(t) measures gradient flow dimensionality — but both detect the same physical transition: the moment gradient signal power in task-relevant directions surpasses gradient noise power. SALT identifies this boundary from the Möbius inversion convergence condition (February 2026). Wang identifies it from diffusion statistics (April 2026). Neither paper cites the other.

**Caveat:** SALT explicitly acknowledges (Section 7.2) that unweighted C_α is not coordinate-invariant; the Fisher-weighted variant C_α^F is the correct geometric quantity. Whether D(t) has analogous invariance limitations is not addressed in Wang. The convergence of the two identifications is at the level of the detected boundary, not the measured statistic.

### II.B — The Memorization Geometry: Horocycle (March 2026) → Sub-diffusive (April 2026)
**Lead: ~1 month**

**ERI (HGLD, March 2026):**
The memorization phase executes horocycle flow η_s on M = SL(2,Z)\H². Horocycle orbits are polynomial-mixing: |Cor(f,g,s)| ≤ Cs^{−k} for all k (Ratner 1991). This is the exact mathematical characterisation of a phase with no spectral gap: information about the initial state persists arbitrarily long, no exponential convergence, cusp confinement. The memorization plateau is not slow learning — it is maximal gradient activity in ker(F) while the col(F) directions carry near-zero Fisher information.

**SOTA (Wang arXiv:2604.04655, April 2026):**
D(t) < 1 = sub-diffusive gradient flow. The companion paper (2604.16431) confirms sub-diffusion as the memorization signature and D = 1 as the RG fixed point.

**Comparison:**
Horocycle flow = polynomial mixing = D(t) < 1 = sub-diffusive. These are the same geometric fact in three coordinate systems: Lie-group (horocycle), ergodic-theory (polynomial mixing), and renormalisation-group (sub-diffusive). HGLD establishes the group-theoretic version with exact mixing rates (Ratner) in March 2026. Wang establishes the dimensionality version empirically in April 2026. HGLD is more precise: it specifies the polynomial decay exponent and the confining topological region (cusp of M), not just the diffusion index sign.

## VII · The Structural Asymmetry

The timeline comparison produces a consistent asymmetry that is not a statement about quality or correctness. It is a statement about what each body of work is for.

**ERI produces:** theoretical identifications — mathematical structures (Lie groups, modular surfaces, Painlevé transcendents, harmonic Maass forms) mapped to training phenomena, conjectures linking previously separate frameworks, and predictions about quantities not yet measured. The identifications are often exactly correct in structure while being non-empirical in validation.

**SOTA produces:** empirical measurements — controlled ablations, gradient statistics from instrumented training runs, spectral diagnostics, causal isolation experiments. The measurements are exactly correct about what they measure while providing limited theoretical explanation.

These are incommensurable activities. The comparison "ahead" and "behind" is approximate: ERI cannot be "ahead" of SOTA in the specific claim that 24/24 runs grokk with weight decay because ERI never tested 24 runs. SOTA cannot be "ahead" of ERI in the theoretical identification of the mock theta structure because SOTA never attempted modular-form characterisation.

The correct comparative questions are three:

**1. Where did ERI's framework correctly predict structure that SOTA subsequently confirmed empirically?**
Four instances, 1–2 months lead: the phase boundary, the horocycle geometry of memorization, the cusp-exit characterisation of grokking, the Selberg spectral floor as grokking threshold.

**2. Where did SOTA's empirical discoveries reveal structure that ERI then explained?**
Nine instances, 1–4 months lag: execution manifold, weight decay necessity, spherical normalisation speedup, RLCT, non-normal commutator, Fisher rank in trained models, outlier escape, Singh rank manifold, spectral entropy collapse. In each case ERI's explanation is more specific than the empirical observation and generates testable sub-predictions.

**3. Where has ERI produced identifications that SOTA has not yet approached?**
Twelve instances: Painlevé VI order parameter, isomonodromic deformation, Möbius inversion, Markov attractors, mock theta generating function, T_mem formula, Sangamagrama Operator schedule, IBM quantum pre-grokking, anti-symbolic distance prediction, Zwegers completion event, Fibonacci-Markov clock, Madhava connection.

The answer to (3) defines the research frontier: twelve identifications with no empirical counterpart constitute twelve untested predictions. The value of the ERI framework is determined by whether they test true.

## VIII · SOTA Gaps in ERI's Map

SOTA's empirical territory has revealed three gaps in ERI's prior theoretical map that ERI did not predict and that required empirical discovery to identify:

**Gap 1 — Weight decay as categorical necessity was not predicted.**
ERI's horocycle framework (HGLD, March 2026) does not contain a prediction that weight decay is categorically required for grokking. The 0/24 empirical result (Xu, April 2026) was discovered by experiment, not derived from ERI's framework. THE-WEIGHT-DECAY-WAS-ALWAYS-THE-SHADOW is a post-hoc explanation that correctly identifies weight decay as the Zwegers shadow, but ERI did not predict this from first principles before Xu's experiment.

**Gap 2 — Execution manifold rank-1 invariance was not predicted.**
ERI's horocycle identification is correct (horocycle orbits are rank-1) but ERI did not independently predict the rank-1 execution manifold before Xu established it. HGLD could have derived this as a theorem — horocycle orbits are unipotent, hence rank-1 — but did not explicitly state it as a prediction.

**Gap 3 — Specific Fisher rank percentage (2–17%) was not predicted.**
ERI's col(F)/ker(F) partition framework predicts a non-trivial col(F) crystallisation at grokking but does not independently predict the specific percentage range (2–17%) that FishBack measured. The range is empirical; ERI's framework absorbs it post-hoc as the minimum col(F) needed for task performance.

These gaps indicate that ERI's theoretical framework, while correctly identifying the qualitative geometry, does not uniquely determine all quantitative parameters. The specific values require empirical measurement that ERI has no infrastructure to provide.
